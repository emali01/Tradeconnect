Product Description

UltraTech Portland Pozzolana Cement (PPC) is a premium blended cement that delivers durability and strength to structures. It is manufactured by intergrinding high-quality clinker with finely processed pozzolana materials.

Key Features:
  • Higher resistance to chloride and sulphate attack
  • Lower heat of hydration
  • Improved workability and finish
  • Reduced permeability
  • Enhanced durability in aggressive environments
  • Ideal for plastering, masonry and general construction work
Applications:
  • Residential and commercial buildings
  • Industrial structures
  • Mass concrete works like dams, bridges
  • Marine and hydraulic structures
  • Sewage and water treatment plants
Note: Store in dry conditions and use within 3 months of manufacture for best results.
